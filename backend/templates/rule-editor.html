How Rules Work

Rules are checked in order. The first matching rule wins — the email gets that label.

Match conditions use OR logic — if From OR Subject OR Body matches, the rule fires.

Leave a field blank to ignore it. At least one field must be filled.

Emails that don't match any rule are passed to AI for labeling.
